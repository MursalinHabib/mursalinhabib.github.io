Gas Grill Instructions

Safe operation guidelines for the outdoor gas grill

üî•

Grill Instructions:

  • Turn on the timer valve to your liking (1 minute - 60 minutes).
  • Open the yellow gas valve.
  • Turn on the gas grill as you would in any gas grill.
  • Igniter works, but if it rains, then you can use the lighter (kept in the kitchen drawer).
  • If you need more time, just rotate the gas valve timer
  • If you are done before your anticipated time, rotate the gas valve timer to zero to turn it off

Hot Tub Instructions

Guidelines for safe and enjoyable hot tub use

üå°Ô∏è

Hot Tub Instructions

  • Remove the tub cover
  • Open the grey control box (Check the detailed Instruction below)
  • Start the water pump. The increment knob is in hours, we usually set to 4 hours.
  • Set the desired temperature in the water heater (Max 120F, DO NOT set beyond that, WARNING!! it may cause scalding). Water takes a bit to get hot (depending on the ambient temperature). Many also enjoy the cold water during hot summer days, in that case set the temperature to 59F.
  • The pool net, pool vacuum are kept in the pantry, if you want to clean it. The hot tub is cleaned between turn over
  • Avoid sitting on/close to the intake and outtake water
  • Test water with your hand before entering
  • Shower before entering to remove lotions/oils
  • Limit sessions to 15-20 minutes
  • Stay hydrated - keep water nearby
  • Put back the cover when not in use.

Health & Safety

Hot tub use is at your own risk. Do not use if you have heart conditions, are pregnant, or under the influence of alcohol or medications. Children must be supervised at all times. Emergency contact information is posted nearby.
