Gas Grill Instructions

Safe operation guidelines for the outdoor gas grill

üî•

Before You Start

  • Check that propane tank is connected and has gas
  • Ensure grill grates are clean and in place
  • Open grill lid before lighting
  • Check for any gas leaks around connections
  • Keep fire extinguisher nearby (located in kitchen)
  • Never use grill indoors or in enclosed spaces
üîß

Lighting the Grill

  • Turn on propane tank valve (counter-clockwise)
  • Open grill lid completely
  • Turn one burner control to HIGH
  • Press ignition button or use long lighter
  • Once lit, turn on additional burners as needed
  • Allow grill to preheat for 10-15 minutes
üßΩ

After Use & Cleaning

  • Turn off all burner controls
  • Close propane tank valve (clockwise)
  • Let grill cool completely before cleaning
  • Clean grates with grill brush (provided)
  • Wipe down exterior with damp cloth
  • Cover grill when not in use

Safety Reminder

Never leave the grill unattended while in use. Always ensure the grill is completely turned off and cool before leaving the area. Report any issues with the grill immediately to the host.
