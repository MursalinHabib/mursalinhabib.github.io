Getting Here:

    If you are driving:
  • Easy access from I-5, I-8, I-15, S-163
  • If you are coming from West on Washington St, Turn left on 1st Ave (Just after the library, Panda Express is on 1st Ave). If you are coming from East, Turn right on 1st Ave (Just after the UPS store/gas station, Panda Express is on the 1st Ave).
  • Turn left on Montecito Way (after two blocks). Then turn left on Front St.
  • The first house on the Front St is your stay :). The turquoise door is hard to miss.
  • Please don't park on the street (It requires special parking permit, we are in process of obtaining it)
  • Park on the driveway. Ample space for three cars.
    • If you are taking a bus:
  • Take bus 3 (UCSD Hospital - Euclid Transit Center and get off on Front St/Arbor Dr (UCSD) near the UCSD Hospital
  • If you are coming from airport, take 992 to downtown bus depot and take 3 to UCSD Hospital
  • Follow the check in instruction for next steps
    • If you are taking taxi/ridesharing:
  • Ask them to take you to UCSD Medical Center (or Give them the address)
  • Follow the check in instruction for next steps
